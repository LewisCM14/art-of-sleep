Martin Mattress Co.

Established in 2006, the Martin Mattress Company is a British mattress manufacturer, with all products being made in the UK, to meet the British Standard Kitemark.


Dedicated to helping our customers get the best night's sleep we can, all our products are hypoallergenic and free from harmful chemical emissions.


Also made from sustainable recourses and delivered in eco-friendly packaging, coming with a seven-year warranty.


Our primary goal is to educate our customers on how, the way they sleep, can impact upon their health and recommend the best products we offer to help improve their sleep-health.
