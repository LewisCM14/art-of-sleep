A guide to a better nights sleep

How do you sleep ?

Back

Do you consider yourself a back-sleeper? A position experts refer to as The Supine Position.


Do you lie on your back with your arms down close to your body in The Soldier Position.


Or do you sleep on your back with your arms above your head in The Starfish Position.

Side

Perhaps you sleep on your side with one arm outstretched in The Yearner Position.


Or do you sleep on your side with your arms down next to your body in The Log Position.


Do you curl up on your side with your knees bent in The Fetal Position.


Or perhaps you and your partner prefer to embrace in The Spooning Position.

Front

Do you sleep flat on your stomach with your face pressed into your pillow and arms along your side in The Prone Position.


Or perhaps you lie flat with your head turned to the side, hugging your pillow, in The Freefall Position.
